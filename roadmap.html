Phase 1 — Discovery & Plan

▼
Strategy & Foundation
We'll start by understanding your current site's strengths and weaknesses, then develop a clear strategy for the new design. This phase includes technical auditing, homepage vision development, and creating a comprehensive sitemap that guides the entire project.
  • Technical audit of current site performance and SEO health
  • Homepage wireframes and user journey mapping
  • Complete sitemap with content strategy and navigation structure
5 days Foundation

Phase 2 — Build & Integrate

▼
Development & IDX Integration
This is where your vision becomes reality. We'll build a custom, mobile-responsive website with full IDX/MLS integration, including saved searches, advanced filters, lead capture forms, and interactive property previews. Every feature is designed to convert visitors into leads.
  • Custom UI framework with mobile-first responsive design
  • Full IDX integration with saved searches, filters, and lead capture
  • Interactive property tours, 3D walkthroughs, and scheduling tools
10 days Core Development

Phase 3 — Content & QA

▼
Content Population & Testing
With the technical foundation in place, we'll populate your site with compelling content, professional photography, and optimized listings. This phase includes comprehensive testing for accessibility, mobile responsiveness, and SEO preparation to ensure your site performs at its best.
  • Content population including photos, copy, and property listings
  • Comprehensive QA testing for accessibility and mobile responsiveness
  • SEO optimization with metadata, Open Graph tags, and schema markup
5 days Polish & Test

Phase 4 — Launch & Handoff

▼
Deployment & Support
The final phase brings your new site live with full analytics tracking, search console setup, and comprehensive admin training. We'll provide post-launch support to ensure smooth operation and help you maximize the site's performance for lead generation.
  • Site deployment with Google Analytics and Search Console setup
  • Admin training and content management system handoff
  • 30-day post-launch support and performance optimization
3-4 days Go Live
